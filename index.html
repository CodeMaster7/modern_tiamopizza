PIZZERIA TI AMO ISN’T JUST A RESTAURANT. IT’S A MASTER CLASS IN THE ART OF MAKING PIZZA, A TRIP THROUGH ITALY’S REGIONS AND A MAGNET FOR A DIVERSE CROWD OF HUNGRY DINERS.

Recognized as one of the best pizzerias in Utah, Pizzeria Ti Amo is known for its local ingredients and a much-talked-about crust keeping locals and visitors alike coming back. Ti Amo wood Fired Pizza is about quality, service, and an appreciation of the italian cuisine. Pizzas are prepared and baked on-site in our wood fired oven. We take our pizza making seriously, and go to extreme measures to provide an authentic product that we are proud of and hope you enjoy!

Ti Amo Wood Fired Pizza is founded on the principles of creating a great customer experience, serving authentic Italian quality pizza to our guests. We believe that it is a privilege to have you come to our pizzeria so we take every detail of your experience into consideration. Our goal is to make our pizzeria your first choice when considering authentic Italian dining. Follow us on facebook!.
